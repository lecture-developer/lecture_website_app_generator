A one-stop-shop solution for all your academic needs

Access and share everything you need from one convenient personal website: publications, course materials, information for research students, side projects, online class recordings and much more. It’s customizable, accessible and available on desktop and mobile devices.

Simple, easy-to-use and intuitive

We conducted research and analyzed relevant data to design a template that’s easy to customize and simple to maintain. You can upload course materials, share your latest publications or post updates in just a few clicks, while still enjoying a polished, professional result. It’s completely free to create a website with our template, and it’s all open-source too, so anyone can contribute.
