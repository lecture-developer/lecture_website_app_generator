Are you a computer science student at BIU?

If you’re a 3rd year C.S. student at Bar Ilan Univerity who’s looking for a cool final year project, you’ve come to the right place! You can join our open-source initative and work on the following projects:

Website generator (SaaS)

This project requires advanced web skills and an open-mind for auto-code generation. You will develop a website app that manages users, collects information, and generates a full website (maybe even auto-deploy it). The key tools for this are MongoDB, Node.js, and React which will make you a desirable web developer in many companies.

Sphera is a free open-source website template that will vastly improve the communication between lecturers and students. It will provide lecturers and professors with the tools to easily create a professional personal website that contains their research, courses and personal projects.

With much of the academic world operating online due to Covid-19, now is the ideal time to finally create the platform that will change academic life for the better.
