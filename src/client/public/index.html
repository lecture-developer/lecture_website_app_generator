Maintaining an academic online presence is not easy. That's why we created our one-stop-shop solution: a free, customizable and easy-to-use template for a personal website, that contains your entire academic life in one place. And yes, it’s mobile friendly too!

You’ll choose what information to share. We’ll do all the rest.

1 Tell us about yourself, and share your research interests, publications and course information.
2 Get a free personal website that showcases your research and helps your students succeed.
3 Update and maintain your website easily and quickly, anytime and from any device.

A one-stop-shop solution for all your academic needs

Your own personal website to host everything important to you in one place: publications, course materials, information for research students, side projects, online class recordings and much more. It’s customizable, accessible and available on both desktop and mobile devices.

Simple, easy-to-use and intuitive

We conducted research and used relevant data to design a template that’s easy to customize and simple to maintain. You’ll be able to upload course materials, share your latest publications or post updates in just a few clicks, while still enjoying a polished, professional result. It’s completely free to create a website with our template, and it’s all open-source too, so anyone can contribute.
