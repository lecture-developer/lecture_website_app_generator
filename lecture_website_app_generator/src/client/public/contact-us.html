OurProduct

OurProduct is a free open-source website template that will vastly improve the communicationbetween lecturers and students. It will provide lecturers and professors with the tools to easily create a professional personal website that contains their research, courses and personal projects.

With much of the academic world operating online due to Covid-19, now is the ideal time to finally create the platform that will change academic life for the better.
